Fun Facts About Software Development 🚀

Did you know?

1. The first computer bug was an actual bug! 🐛
   In 1947, Grace Hopper found a moth causing problems in the Harvard Mark II computer.

2. The term "debugging" came from removing that moth.

3. The first programmer was Ada Lovelace 👩‍💻
   She wrote the first algorithm intended for processing on a computer.

4. Linux's mascot Tux the penguin was chosen because Linus Torvalds was once bitten by a penguin! 🐧

5. The first website is still online! 
   You can visit it at: info.cern.ch
